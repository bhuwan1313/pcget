MOTHERBOARD

Gigabyte H610

Gigabyte H610 motherboards are entry-level, micro-ATX or mini-ITX form factor boards designed for Intel's Alder Lake and Raptor Lake processors, offering a cost-effective option for building mainstream and gaming PCs. They support dual-channel DDR4 memory, have limited expansion slots, and offer features like M.2 for NVMe storage and Smart Fan 6 for cooling.

     

SSD

Crucial 1TB nvme

The Crucial P3 1TB NVMe SSD is a PCIe 3.0 NVMe M.2 SSD offering high speeds and storage capacity for desktops and laptops. It boasts read speeds up to 3500MB/s and write speeds up to 3000MB/s. The drive uses Micron Advanced 3D NAND technology and is available in a 2280 form factor.

     

SMPS

Ant esports VS500L

The Ant Esports VS500L is a 500W non-modular power supply designed for gaming and entry-to-mid-level PC builds. It features a single 12V rail, a 120mm silent fan, and modern connectors, making it a good option for budget-conscious gamers.

     

RAM

16GB DDR4

Clock Speeds: DDR4 RAM is available in various clock speeds, typically ranging from 2133 MHz to 3200 MHz. High-performance modules can reach even higher frequencies, such as 4266 MHz or 4800 MHz. Capacity: Common DDR4 module sizes include 4GB, 8GB, 16GB, and 32GB. Some specialized modules offer up to 64GB per stick.

     

Processor

Intel i7 12th generation

The Intel Core i7 12th generation, codenamed Alder Lake, is a line of desktop and mobile processors known for their hybrid architecture with Performance-cores and Efficient-cores. They support DDR4 and DDR5 memory, PCIe Gen 5, and feature Intel UHD Graphics 770 on some models.

     

Cabinet

Zebronics zeb-cronous

The Zebronics Zeb-Cronus is a premium gaming cabinet, offering a mid-tower form factor, compatible with ATX and mATX motherboards. It features a mirror-finish front, tempered glass panels on the front and side, and 4 x 120mm Rainbow LED double ring fans, contributing to its aesthetic appeal and cooling capabilities. The design also includes optimized airflow, a top-mounted PSU, and a heavy-duty structure.
